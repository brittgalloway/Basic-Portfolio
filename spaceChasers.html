Space Chasers

Space Chasers is a web application that allows users in the USA to quickly know if they are able to find and view an Aurora. The user inputs a zipcode and the app finds the the nearest registered Dark Sky Places via the International Dark-Sky Association (IDA). It takes things a step further by providing the weather (you need clear sky conditions), sunset and sunrise (so you don't get there too early or too late), and directions to said location. I worked on a team to bring this app to life. I was the lead designer on the project and created everything you see.
Our Github has a list of features we want to add and improve.
