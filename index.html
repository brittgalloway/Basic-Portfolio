About Me

Hi! I'm Brittney. I am an animator with a strong interest in interactive media and web developement. I went to Califorina College of the Arts in 2010 and graduated in 2014 with my BFA in Animation. I've always loved art and design, and while I love the films and products from Walt Disney Studios and Studio Ghibli and many others, I'm not really interested in working at a major animation company. I've found more fulfillment in interactive e-books, animated shorts and web-developement. Potentionally getting into game design and developement as well. When I am not animating or fumbling through code, I am drawing or playing Horizon Zero Dawn!
