About Me

  • Name: Brittney L. Galloway
  • Location: Greater Chicagoland Area
  • Skills: HTML, CSS, BOOTSTRAP, Materialize, JavaScript, Firebase
  • Hobbies: Tennis, AfroPunk, all things animation, art, & video games
I am an animator with a strong interest in interactive media and web developement. I graduated from Califorina College of the Arts in 2014 with my BFA in Animation. I've always loved art and design, and while I love the films and products from Walt Disney Studios, Studio Ghibli and many others, I'm not really interested in working at a major animation company. I have found more fulfillment in interactive e-books, animated shorts and web-developement. Potentionally getting into game design and developement as well. I already have a website for my art and animation at Sleepy Gallows. I think it's important to note that the webseries I'm creating with my sister looks a lot like the walk through of a video game. The interest has always been there! When I am not animating or fumbling through code, I am playing Horizon Zero Dawn or an obscure PS2 remaster for PS4.
